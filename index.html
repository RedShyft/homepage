How Does It Work?

Define your target audience

The first step is to define who you want to target on Reddit. You do this by entering keywords or phrases that are used to find users talking about certain subjects. You can drill down further by targeting specific subreddit communities.

Craft a personalized message

Once you've defined your audience, you write a message to be sent to users who match your criteria. Messages can be personalized with username, subreddit name or matched keyword.

Enjoy the magic of automation

Now it's time to put your marketing efforts on auto-pilot. RedShyft will search Reddit for posts matching your target audience and send the author your message. Since your Reddit account is connected, any replies you receive can be responded to through Reddit.
